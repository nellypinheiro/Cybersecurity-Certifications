## Overview
This Skill Check Lab focused on reconnaissance, footprinting, and scanning techniques as part of the initial phase of a penetration testing engagement. The objective was to identify exposed services, misconfigurations, and publicly accessible information that could expand the targetâ€™s attack surface.

The lab required applying both active and passive reconnaissance techniques without guided solutions, reinforcing independent analysis and methodology.

---

## Skills Practiced
- Reconnaissance and attack surface mapping
- Service and port enumeration
- Identification of exposed services and misconfigurations
- Analysis of network-accessible resources
- Prioritization of findings for later exploitation phases

---

## Tools Used
- Nmap
- FTP
- MySQL client utilities

---

## Key Learning Outcomes
- Improved structured approach to footprinting and scanning
- Hands-on experience identifying exposed network services
- Better understanding of how small misconfigurations expand attack surfaces
- Reinforced importance of recon accuracy before exploitation
