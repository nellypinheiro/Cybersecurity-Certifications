# Assessment Methodologies: Information Gathering CTF 1

**Platform:** INE / eLearnSecurity  
**Certification Path:** eJPT  
**Type:** Skill Check Lab (Graded)  
**Focus Area:** Information Gathering & Reconnaissance  

---

## Overview
This Skill Check Lab focused on information gathering and reconnaissance techniques against a target web application. The objective was to identify publicly accessible information, misconfigurations, and exposed resources that could assist in further penetration testing phases.

Unlike guided labs, Skill Check Labs are graded and do not provide solutions, requiring independent problem-solving and investigative skills.

---

## Skills Practiced
- Web reconnaissance methodology
- Open-source information gathering (OSINT concepts)
- Identification of exposed files and directories
- Detection of misconfigurations in web environments
- Critical analysis of publicly accessible information

---

## Tools Used
- Firefox
- Curl
- HTTrack

---

## Key Learning Outcomes
- Strengthened structured approach to information gathering
- Improved ability to identify valuable reconnaissance data
- Hands-on experience analyzing real-world web misconfigurations
- Reinforced importance of recon in the attack lifecycle
